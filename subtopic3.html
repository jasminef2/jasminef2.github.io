 The Effect of Medical Technology Innovations on Patient Outcomes, 1990-2015: Results of a Physician Survey
Site Author
 David E Wamble, Michael Ciarametaro, Robert Dubois
Abstract
 This text describes how medical technological innovations have affected patients from the physicians' perspective. It explains how the development of diagnostics, medical devices, procedures, and more have vastly improved quality of life and increased life expectancy for many different health issues. The text also goes into the importance of supporting and funding new innovations such as pharmaceuticals or biopharmaceuticals.
