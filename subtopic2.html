The Evolution of Medical Technology

URL
Medical Devices Timeline
Site Author
 Morgridge Institute for Research


Abstract
 Site contains a basic timeline of the beginnings of medical technology until the present day. Includes years and a brief description of what was discovered and what it was used for. The article starts with the year 1881 and the invention of the device to measure blood pressure to 1952 and the development of the first successful cardiac pacemaker. The article goes all the way to 2009 with the development of an advanced prosthetic arm. It also describes who developed and discovered these devices.

 Medical devices and technology across the years
Site Author
 Yale Medicine Magazine
Abstract
 This is also a timeline of the evolution of technology in medicine. However, this article includes advancements in medical devices dating back to 1816 and the invention of the stethoscope. This also includes other technological advancements that were not mentioned in the other timeline, such as the merging of computer technology with medical technology in the 1970s and the development and implantation of the first artificial heart in 1982.
